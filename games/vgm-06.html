Junkbot

Junkbot is a LEGO-themed puzzle game that was playable on LEGO.com from 2001 to 2012. It was brought back by Flashpoint and is now available to play on Venison. Players move and stack blocks to guide the titular Junkbot to garbage cans in as few moves as possible.
(Not a Venison original)


Since this is not a Venison original game, this version has no expiration date.
